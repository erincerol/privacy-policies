Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Data Collection

The Bake Log is fully offline and stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any servers.

What Data Is Stored

Your recipes, bake sessions, and starter logs are stored in a local SQLite database on your device. This data never leaves your phone.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

Firebase Crashlytics may collect anonymized crash reports to help us improve app stability. No personal data is included in these reports.

Ads

Google AdMob may collect limited data for ad targeting on the free tier. Upgrade to Premium to remove all ads.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed through Google Play (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS). We do not handle payment data directly.

Your Rights

All your data is yours. You can export or delete it anytime by uninstalling the app. We have no central record of your data.
